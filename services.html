Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our tattoo services.

How much does a tattoo cost?

Tattoo pricing varies based on size, complexity, placement, and the artist's experience. Small tattoos start at ₹1000, while larger custom pieces can range from ₹3000 to ₹20000+. During your consultation, we'll provide a detailed quote based on your specific design.

How long does a tattoo take to heal?

Surface healing typically takes 2-3 weeks, but complete healing (all layers of skin) can take 4-6 weeks. Proper aftercare is essential for optimal healing and tattoo longevity. We provide detailed aftercare instructions and are available to answer any healing questions.

Do you use sterile equipment?

Absolutely. We maintain the highest standards of hygiene and safety. All needles are single-use and disposed of immediately after each session. Our studio undergoes regular sterilization, and our artists follow strict health protocols to ensure a safe environment for every client.

Can I bring my own design?

Yes! We welcome custom designs and will work with you to adapt your concept for optimal tattooing. Our artists can also create original designs based on your ideas, references, or inspiration. During your consultation, we'll discuss your vision and create a design that works well as a tattoo.

How do I care for my new tattoo?

We provide detailed aftercare instructions specific to your tattoo. Generally, this includes keeping the tattoo clean, applying recommended ointment, avoiding sun exposure, and not picking at healing skin. We also offer aftercare products for purchase and are always available to answer healing questions.
